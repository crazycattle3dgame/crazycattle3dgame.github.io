About Crazy Cattle 3D

Crazy Cattle 3D is a fun 3D action arcade where you herd a mischievous cattle crew across obstacle-filled maps. Race the clock, dodge traps, use boosts, and unlock upgrades to finish levels with the highest score — all running smoothly in your browser, no downloads needed.

Our Story

Crazy Cattle 3D started as a passion project to deliver a lightweight, fast, and accessible 3D arcade experience. We focus on responsive controls, diverse maps, progressive difficulty, and playful mechanics like herding, ragdoll collisions, and helpful power-ups. Today, the game is enjoyed by players worldwide who frequently share ideas and feedback.
